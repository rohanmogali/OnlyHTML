Knowing the status and accreditation of the college or institute is one of the foremost concerns of the admission seeker. It is important to know the kinds of universities, their status and degrees conferred by the education institutes.

There are different kinds of Universities which offer Engineering degree namely,

1.Central Universities
A central university is established by an Act of Parliament and are under the direct purview of the Department of Higher Education (DHE) under Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD).

2.State Universities
As the name suggests, state universities are established, run and funded by the state government. State universities are usually established by a local legislative assembly act.

3.Deemed to be Universities
“Deemed” is a status of autonomy granted to a university by DHE under MHRD, on the advice of UGC. Deemed to be Universities enjoy full autonomy or independence in deciding courses, syllabus, admissions and fees. Some Deemed to be Universities can also grant degrees on their own. Deemed-to-be Universities, which continue to perform well, can get the status of a full-fledged University.

4.Private Universities
Private universities are also UGC approved institutes. However, such universities do not run on central or state funds. They can grant degrees but cannot have off-campus colleges or affiliate an institution/college. The Private universities need to adhere to UGC rules and regulations for operations. The UGC regularly sends committees to inspect Private universities.

The possible answer to this question might be that sooner the completion of degree, lesser will be the competition for jobs. Hence the person can get exposure to jobs, industries and market at the earliest.
But Why 4 years??

First year of the engineering degree is dedicated to study basics, which is common for all the students irrespective of the branch chosen. Second year is for core subjects of the branch chosen. Third year is for the study of elective subjects opted by student. Fourth year is for application where the theoretical knowledge is applied to real-time projects, internship offers and placements.
